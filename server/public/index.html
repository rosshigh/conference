SAP Academic Alliance Conference - North America 2020

Co-hosted by the University Competence Centers and SAP Next-Gen Chapters

Professors from SAP University Alliances / SAP Next-Gen member institutions are invited to attend the SAP University Alliance Conference on July 20 - 21, 2020 and SAP University Alliances Boot Camps on July 22 - 24, 2020.

The events are organized and co-hosted by the University Competence Centers and SAP Next-Gen Chapters. To cover costs associated with the conference, UW-Milwaukee charges a fee for registering and attending.

Networking Event

The conference concludes with an opportunity to network over dinner at The Domes. Come help us celebrate a new beginning for our community.

Experience a desert oasis, a tropical jungle and floral gardens…all at one destination only minutes from downtown Milwaukee!
